About Me

To be a writer of children’s books has been my dream for many years. It quite takes my breath away when I imagine myself daring to join the realms of those esteemed heroes, whose words have nourished my soul? I am compelled to write. Writing ignites my soul and makes me feel alive.

Here is my secret. It is very simple. It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye.

Shirin Shamsi

Author of Children’s Books
